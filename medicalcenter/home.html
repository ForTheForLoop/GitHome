Welcome to Carlow Medical Center

Welcome to Carlow Medical Center, a modern, dynamic practice providing a full range of general practice care to public and private patients.

We are a Family run medical center dating back to the 1900's and we thrive to help everybody.

We are proud to say we won the Leinster Medical center of the year in 2015.
